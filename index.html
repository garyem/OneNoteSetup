🚀Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1️⃣ Open OneNote
Find it in your apps or Office 365
2️⃣ Create Notebook
File → New → Name it "Work Notes"
3️⃣ Add Section
Right-click → New Section → "Meetings"
4️⃣ Start Typing!
Click anywhere and begin your notes

🆘 Meeting Starting in 30 seconds?

1. Open OneNote → 2. Press Ctrl + N for new page → 3. Type meeting name → 4. Start recording in Teams → 5. Take notes!
Transcript will auto-appear - focus on key decisions and action items!

🔧Working with Transcripts

🎯 Find Action Items
Search transcript for "need to", "will do", "action", "follow up"
📝 Extract Key Quotes
Copy important statements directly from transcript to your summary
⏰ Jump to Moments
Click timestamp in transcript to hear that exact part of recording
👤 Track Speakers
See who said what - perfect for assigning action items
💡 Remember: Click anywhere on the page to start typing. OneNote is like a digital paper - totally flexible!
